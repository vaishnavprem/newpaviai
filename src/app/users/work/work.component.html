How Pavi Works

The PAVI experience gives users a unique experience that provides insight and transparency around the interview process, the ability to get real-time feedback on presentation, demeanor, communication and expression allowing opportunity to grow their skill-set when it comes to presenting to others.

The PAVI platform offers employers a tremendous opportunity to enhance their candidates job seeking experience while assessing the candidate on a level playing field.

Step 1- You will receive an invitation to create a user account. Simply follow the steps to create the account and login to the PAVI platform.

Step 2- Once logged in, you will be able to view the job you have been invited to apply to. Click “get started” to begin the interview process.

Step 3- 

You will be prompted with questions that are specific to the job you are interviewing for. Please watch the timer, each question has a set time for your response!

Step 4- 

That’s it! You’re done and you’re welcome to send us feedback on the process via the link on the page.

Employers! Once you login to your personal dashboard, you will see all your uniquely qualified "PAVI Picks" that are the right fit for your organization.
